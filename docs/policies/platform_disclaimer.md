# Platform Scope & Financial Disclaimer

**Effective Date**: January 1, 2026  
**Product**: ChitVault Digital Ledger

## 1. Professional Record-Keeping Only
ChitVault is a specialized **Digital Ledger** designed for the professional administration and auditing of auction chit funds. It provides mathematical models, data isolation, and reporting tools to assist Finance Firms in their operations.

## 2. No Payment Processing
> [!IMPORTANT]
> **ChitVault is NOT a payment gateway or a financial institution.**  
> The platform does not handle, process, or hold any actual currency. It does not integrate with bank APIs to facilitate transfers. 

All financial activities, including but not limited to:
- Collection of monthly subscriptions
- Payout of auction proceeds
- Payment of dividends

**MUST occur externally** via cash, bank transfer, or other legal financial instruments. The "Payment" module within ChitVault serves exclusively as a digital record of these external transactions.

## 3. Accuracy & Audit Responsibility
While ChitVault provides automated calculations (Dividends, Commissions, etc.), the accuracy of the ledger is entirely dependent on the data entered by the Firm Owner and their Staff. 
- Firm Owners are responsible for verifying that every digital record corresponds to a physical transaction.
- We recommend weekly reconciliation between the ChitVault **Cashbook** and actual bank/office statements.

## 4. Physical Record Primacy (Legal Truth)
In any legal dispute, audit, or verification process:
- The **Primary Truth** remains the collection of physical signed vouchers, bank statements, and office registers.
- ChitVault serves as a **secondary digital reconciliation tool** and does not replace the requirement for legitimate physical financial records required by local law.

## 5. Limitation of Liability
The providers of ChitVault shall not be held liable for:
- Financial discrepancies resulting from incorrect data entry.
- Mismanagement of funds by the Finance Firm.
- Legal disputes between the Firm and its members.

By using this platform, you acknowledge that you are using a bookkeeping tool and maintain full legal responsibility for your firm's actual financial transactions.
